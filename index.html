The way modern technology helps a philologist

The newest technology has simplified the work of philologists. Firstly, thanks to the Internet, the specialists of the mentioned area can search for the necessary books and articles without wasting time visiting the library. We can also access ancient archieves which are far from our region using differet websites.Philologists no longer need to make efforts to write a bibliography because computers can do it. They no longer need to look for mistakes in the text because computers also can do it. In other words, with the advent of technology we got rid of most of the problems arise due to the human factor. Modern computer technologies are imperfect but they significantly facilitate the work in the scientific field.
